A dry home isn‘t just uncomfortable, it can aggravate allergies, dry out skin, and even increase the chance of catching a cold. A good humidifier adds moisture back into the air, helping you breathe easier and sleep better. But you don‘t have to spend a fortune to get relief. Budget-friendly models can still be quiet, easy to clean, and effective as long as you match the tank size and coverage to your room. When shopping on a budget, focus on three things: tank capacity (larger means fewer refills), noise level (ultrasonic cool mist models tend to be whisper-quiet), and ease of cleaning. A wide-mouth top-fill design or a demineralization cartridge can save you headaches later. Choosing the right budget humidifier comes down to where you‘re using it and how much maintenance you‘re willing to do.
